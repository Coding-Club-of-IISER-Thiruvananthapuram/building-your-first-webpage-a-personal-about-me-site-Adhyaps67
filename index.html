About Me

Hi, I'm Adhya. I am a first-year undergraduate student at IISER Thiruvananthapuram.

I am from Trivandrum, Kerala. In my free time, I enjoy drawing — especially portraits of personalities. I also like to experiment with art using MS Paint.

After joining IISER, I picked up photography. I've covered around 33 events, including ISHYA, as part of the photography team.

I’m currently doing an internship under Prof. Rajeev Kini in the Ultrafast Spectroscopy Lab. My work involves making presentations, reading research papers, theses, and review articles.

In the future, I’d like to explore the ultrafast behavior of materials in greater depth.
